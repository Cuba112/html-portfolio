David's TV Shows List

Top TV Shows Of All Time

Game Of Thrones

Packed with intense action, suspense, and unexpected turns in the storyline, this series unravels the ascension to power of (Jon Snow), the illegitimate son of Eddard Stark, who emerged victorious after facing exile. It narrates a saga involving dragons, a conflict among mighty kings, and a flamboyant display of authority.

Breaking Bad

The challenges faced by a financially strained high school teacher grappling with a cancer diagnosis, as he resorts to unlawful means to secure an inheritance for his family. Alongside him is his former student, contending with a battle against drug addiction.

The Wire

"The Wire" follows a special task force dedicated to unveiling the identities behind a drug cartel in their city. The series delves into the complex interplay between law enforcement, politics, and the streets, portraying a gritty and realistic portrayal of Baltimore. With a focus on both sides of the law, it explores the challenges and intricacies of the ongoing battle against drugs in urban society.
